What is NyctoPhobia?
What is Nyctophobia?

Nyctophobia is an extreme fear of the dark, especially being alone in the dark. It can disturb daily tasks and cause insomnia.

About Nyctodetect
About Nyctodetect

This is a project to collect information about Nyctophobia. Our results will be implemented into an AI project provided by Teachable Machine.

End Product
